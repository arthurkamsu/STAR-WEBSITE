We do not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal information from users of our application. Our app operates entirely on your device and does not connect to external servers to transmit or retrieve data.

Our application does not require any special permissions (e.g., camera, location, contacts) to function. This means it does not access or interact with your personal data or device features.
